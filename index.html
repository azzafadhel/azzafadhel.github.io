About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Washington State University, advised by Prof. Jana Doppa and Prof. Nghia Hoang. My research focuses on data-efficient AI methods to accelerate scientific discovery and engineering design in domains where collecting data involves expensive physical experiments or simulations.

I specialize in surrogate modeling and adaptive experimental design, with high-impact applications in nanoporous materials discovery, additive manufacturing, and wearable devices for health monitoring. My work has been published at top-tier AI/ML venues including ICML, AAAI, and AISTATS.

Research Interests
  • Surrogate modeling for offline black-box optimization via gradient matching and meta-learning.
  • AI-driven adaptive experimental design for metal additive manufacturing (3D printing).
  • Nanoporous materials discovery via search bias-guided surrogate modeling.
  • Multi-objective, multi-fidelity Bayesian optimization for wearable health devices.

Research Projects

Materials Discovery
Surrogate Modeling for Offline Black-Box Optimization
ML surrogates from small offline datasets via gradient-matching and meta-learning with Gaussian-process synthetic task generation. See Learning Surrogates via Gradient Matching (ICML 2024) and Black-Box Optimization via Meta Learning (AISTATS 2026).
🏗️
Additive Manufacturing
AI-Driven Adaptive Experimental Design for Metal 3D Printing
Probabilistic surrogate frameworks for rapid, defect-free printing of NASA's GRCop-42 alloy. See Discovery of Feasible 3D Printing Configurations (AAAI/IAAI 2026).
⚗️
Scientific Discovery
Nanoporous Materials Discovery
Search bias-guided surrogate modeling combining value-matching and optimization-regularized objectives. See Nanoporous Materials Discovery (AAAI AISI 2026).
⌚
Healthcare Wearables
Multi-Objective Bayesian Optimization for Wearable Devices
Multi-fidelity Bayesian optimization to jointly optimize energy efficiency and sensing accuracy for continuous health monitoring. See Wear-DSE (ESWEEK 2026, under review).
